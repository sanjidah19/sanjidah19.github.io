Hi, I am Noor Khan,

Nature's Very Own Photographer

Say Hi to
Noor Khan

Growing up,she didn't have a lot. She was raised in a household of seven in a broken two-room house in India. She had four siblings, three brothers and one sister. The oldest of her siblings, everyone depended on her. Her parents were becoming old and weak, and couldn't take care of her or her siblings. She needed to earn the bread and butter for her family somehow. She started off by cleaning people's shoes. One day, she had earned enough to buy materials to make a self made camera. A woman, fascinated by Noor's intelligence, asked her to become a photo journalist for her. So that's how her career began.

 

Although she started off by taking pictures of what was happening in India around the time, she realized her passion lied within nature. She loved taking pictures of the mountains, volcanoes, plains, the night skies, and wildlife.
